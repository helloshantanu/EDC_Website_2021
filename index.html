OUR Programs

We provides the opportunity to prepare for life

Mentorship

We at EDC, encourage minds with thought provoking ideas and provide them with substantial business opportunities.

Student Run Ventures

Helping young students to ideate, test, build prototype and launch their venture.

Workshops

Workshops are organised - both indoor and outbound, which are learner centered, interactive, activity oriented and lively.

Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps

Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps are held with an objective to enhance knowledge and kindle the skills of the young minds aspiring to be successful entrepreneurs.

Industrial Visit

Industrial Visits are organised where students can learn about production level atmosphere and interact with professionals from the industry.

Incubation

Helps startups bootstrap during its early stages and provide mentoring, guidance, co-working space and also at times some funding.

Our philoshophy

How we do what we do?

1
IDEATE

Ideation is what helps founders and executives determine the right problem to solve and how to solve it.

2
Innovate

Innovation fosters competitive advantage as it allows you to stand out with better products and services.

3
Incubate

Incubation provides resources and environment that allow the very best ideas and entrepreneurs to flourish.
